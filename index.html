About Me

Back in 2018 I began my journey into coding and programming, after a few years planning to be an actor,
thanks to my friend. I began simple with discord bots before I started venturing into HTML soon after.
From then on I took a liking to making websites because it felt like full creative freedom to mess around with code
and such I continued to play around and progress in my new-chosen field.

In 2021 I founded Crimson Pixl LTD, with another friend of mine, making both discord bots and websites for clientele
after spending years in business classes I had achieved a life goal with a team of 4 people that I led.
Owning Crimson Pixl only fueled my passion for coding as I learned new tricks and techniques
to make better products for the clients whilst also taking an interest in games programming as i ventured towards college.

Now i aim my sights on University, learning more knowledge to make my projects better and more exciting along with
learning new languages and tools such as VUE.js and working on other small projects so i can also work on my other passions
of; writing and acting along with filming and editing.
