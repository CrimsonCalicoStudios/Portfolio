About the game

The pre-game:

DeltaStar takes place after your ship, The Contour, Escapes capture from the The Yusha Federation, Unfortunately your support from carmine squadron were unable to make it leaving you on your own with nothing but a damaged ship and a broken hyperdrive.

Lost in Federation space after your engines malfunction you get jumped by a scout ship. Your only hope is to take them out in time for your rescue team arrive on your location.



More about the game:

DeltaStar is a 2v2 space Opera game set in a bleak futuristic authoritarian Society, where are beady-eye protagonist -team 1- must survive the oncoming bonbardment of their enemy empire's goons -team 2. This Space extravigansa game developed for the "co-operation" dome show game project for Leicester College and The National Space Centre. Created by the team behind 3PointStudios, DeltaStar is a 2 player combat game drawing inspiration from multiple games such as; Heavenly Bodies' control system and map.
